From India, Pakistan, or abroad?

The 4 things most plans from outside the UK get wrong

Endorsing bodies aren't biased against you — but they're under pressure to prove "genuineness," so your plan has to work harder. Here's where we see overseas applicants slip up most.

🔁

"Adapting a model" reads as replication

UK endorsing bodies score "adapting a US or India model to the UK" as replication, not innovation. They want clear differentiation against what's already trading in the UK. We flag this risk before you submit.

📊

Higher scrutiny on plans from Pakistan and India

Reported Q1 2022 refusal rates: Pakistan ~40%, India ~24%, overall ~35%. Not a policy bias — endorsing bodies are under more pressure to prove genuineness. Your plan has to work twice as hard to stand out.

🇺🇰

UK market traction is the hardest evidence

Endorsing bodies want UK customer signal, local competitor analysis, and pilot evidence. Hard to gather from Karachi, Lahore, or Bangalore. We tell you specifically where your UK evidence is thin and what to add.

📄

Generic templated plans get spotted instantly

Many low-cost overseas consultants ship templated plans. Endorsing bodies recognise the patterns immediately and reject. We score against the same signals they use — so you catch the "template feel" before they do.
