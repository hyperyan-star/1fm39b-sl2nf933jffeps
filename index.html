Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is this a replacement for an immigration lawyer?
No. This tool reviews your business plan against endorsement criteria — it does not provide immigration legal advice. For legal guidance, work with a qualified immigration solicitor.
How does the AI reviewer work?
Our AI analyses your business plan against the same Innovation, Viability, and Scalability criteria that endorsing bodies use. It's built on real endorsement feedback and the latest 2026 criteria — not a generic AI tool.
What do the free scores mean?
Each score (0–10) reflects how well your plan addresses that criterion. Above 7 typically indicates strong alignment. Below 5 suggests significant gaps likely to be flagged during evaluation.
What's in the full £149 report?
A detailed breakdown of weaknesses for each criterion, specific improvement suggestions, a comparison of how your plan aligns with what UKES, Innovator International, and Envestors look for, and actionable next steps.
Is my business plan data stored or shared?
No. Your plan is processed in real-time and is not stored after analysis. We do not share your data with any third party.
Can I use this if I've already been rejected?
Absolutely. The tool is especially useful for rejected applicants. Upload your revised plan, see where it scores low, and use the full report to understand exactly what to fix.
Which endorsing body should I choose?
The report highlights your plan's strengths and weaknesses against the endorsement criteria used across all three UK endorsing bodies. It doesn't recommend a specific body, but the insights will help you make a more informed choice based on where your plan is strongest.
